Our Mission

The Moore High School Band is a music-making community where students are given the tools and encouragement necessary for group and individual success. Members will utilize determination, pride, discipline, and passion to achieve high musical goals. Our top priority is excellence of product and process.

Stay Connected

Staying connected is an essential part of band. It is required and expected for every student or parent to join the Remind. Payments, band fees, and dues can be paid through Remind. For more information regarding Charms, check the info tab or click here. Don't forget to stay up to date with the calendar. It will be regularly updated with upcoming concerts, contests, and events.
