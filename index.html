Our Mission

The Moore High School Band is a music-making community where students are given the tools and encouragement necessary for group and individual success. Members will utilize determination, pride, discipline, and passion to achieve high musical goals. Our top priorty is excellence of product and process.

Get Connected

Staying connected is an essential part of band. It is required and expected for every student or parent to join the Remind. Payments, band fees, and dues can be paid through Charms. For more information regarding Charms, check the info tab or click here. Don't forget to stay up to date regulary with the calendar. It will be kept up to date with upcoming concerts, contests, events, and other happenings.
